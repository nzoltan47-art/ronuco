Key Patterns in 1–100

Once you spot these patterns, Roman numerals become easy to read and write from memory.

1–9: I, V combos
I=1, II=2, III=3, IV=4, V=5, VI=6, VII=7, VIII=8, IX=9
10–90: X, L combos
X=10, XX=20, XXX=30, XL=40, L=50, LX=60, LXX=70, LXXX=80, XC=90
Subtractive pairs
IV=4, IX=9, XL=40, XC=90 — smaller before larger means subtract
100 = C
C comes from the Latin word "centum" meaning one hundred
