Why Look Up a Year in Roman Numerals?

Years written in Roman numerals appear more often than most people realise.

🎬 Film copyright dates
Movies traditionally show their production year as a Roman numeral in the closing credits — e.g. MMXXIV for 2024.
🖋 Tattoos
Birth years, anniversaries, and meaningful dates are among the most popular Roman numeral tattoo choices.
🏛 Buildings & monuments
Foundation stones, cornerstones, and plaques on public buildings often display the year of construction in Roman numerals.
🎓 Graduation & diplomas
Many universities and formal certificates print the conferral year in Roman numerals as a mark of tradition.
